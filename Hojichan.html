Our Story

Hoji-Chan was born from a simple idea: to take the warm, roasted comfort of Japanese hojicha and infuse it with a joyful, refreshing sparkle. We take premium hojicha, carefully steep it to perfection, and then lightly carbonate it to create a drink that's both familiar and delightfully new.

Inspired by the vibrant and innovative spirit of the San Francisco Bay Area, Hoji-Chan is a celebration of tradition meeting modernity. We're proud to bring this unique, low-caffeine beverage to our local community.

Our Delicious Drinks

The Classic

The original and beloved Hoji-Chan. Pure, nutty, and roasted notes with a crisp, clean finish. The perfect introduction to our brand.

Yuzu-Ginger

A zesty and invigorating blend of bright yuzu citrus and a hint of warm ginger. A delightful and sophisticated sparkle.

Blackberry-Mint

A refreshing and fruity concoction. The sweet, juicy notes of blackberry meet the cool, crisp finish of fresh mint.
